What are the current interest rates for home loans?

Our home loan interest rates start at 8.40% p.a. for salaried individuals and 8.65% p.a. for self-employed applicants. The exact rate depends on:

  • Loan amount and tenure
  • Your credit score and financial profile
  • Property type and location
  • Current market conditions

Use our EMI Calculator to estimate your monthly payments based on different interest rates.

What documents are required for a home loan application?

For Salaried Individuals:

  • Identity proof (PAN Card, Aadhaar, Passport)
  • Address proof (Aadhaar, Utility bills)
  • Last 3 months' salary slips
  • Form 16 or last 2 years' IT returns
  • Last 6 months' bank statements
  • Property documents (if finalized)

For Self-Employed Individuals:

  • Identity and address proof (same as above)
  • Last 3 years' IT returns with profit/loss statements
  • Last 3 years' balance sheets and audit reports
  • Business proof (GST registration, trade license)
  • Bank statements for last 12 months
  • Property documents
What is the maximum amount I can get as a personal loan?

Personal loan amounts range from ₹50,000 to ₹40 lakhs, depending on:

  • Your monthly income (minimum ₹25,000 required)
  • Employment stability (minimum 2 years for salaried)
  • Credit score (minimum 650 recommended)
  • Existing financial obligations

Existing customers with good repayment history may qualify for higher amounts and preferential rates.

What types of business loans do you offer?

We offer a range of business financing solutions:

  • Term Loans: For expansion, equipment purchase (₹5 lakh to ₹2 crore)
  • Working Capital Loans: For daily operations (up to ₹1 crore)
  • MSME Loans: Special schemes for small businesses
  • Startup Loans: For businesses less than 3 years old
  • Loan Against Property: For business needs using property as collateral
  • Invoice Financing: Against unpaid invoices
Do education loans require collateral?

Collateral requirements depend on the loan amount:

  • Up to ₹7.5 lakh: No collateral required
  • ₹7.5 lakh to ₹1.5 crore: Collateral required (property or fixed deposits)

Some premier institutions have special schemes where loans up to ₹20 lakh may not require collateral. We also offer a moratorium period where repayments start only after course completion.

How long does it take to get loan approval?

Approval times vary by loan type:

  • Home Loans: 24 to 72 hours
  • Business Loans: 24 to 48 hours
  • Education Loans: 3 to 5 working days

These are approximate timelines assuming all documents are submitted correctly. Pre-approved customers may get instant approvals.
